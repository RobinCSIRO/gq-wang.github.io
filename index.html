Short Bio

I am currently a PhD candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales Sydney, Australia. My supervisors are Dr. Changming Sun and Prof. Arcot Sowmya. I am an Australian Government Research Training Program (RTP) scholarship holder between 2017-2020.

My current research focuses on designing data-efficient and resource-efficient learning algorithms to solve challenging computer vision and image understanding problems (i.e., generative image modelling, low-level vision problem, fine-grained image classification etc.). Besides, I am also interested in solving basci machine learning problems (i.e., semi-/self-/un-supervised learning, domain adaptation, multi-task learning etc.).
